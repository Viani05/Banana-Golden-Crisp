About Banana Golden Crisp

Welcome to the world of 'delight in every bite'.Banana Golden Crisp has been bringing you the finest quality banana chips since 1984. Our flagship brand is inspired by authentic recipes, perfected over generations.

We are committed to providing fresh, crisp & tasty ready-to-eat snacks by maintaining state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities. Each batch is made from the finest ingredients and hygienically packed to bring out a taste of nostalgia.

With a focus on quality, hygiene, and authentic taste, we have become a well-recognized and highly regarded business entity in the snack food industry.
